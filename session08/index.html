Why We Love Berries

Berries are nature’s candy — sweet, tart, and packed with antioxidants. From strawberries to blueberries to blackberries, these tiny fruits bring color and nutrition to every meal.

They’re rich in vitamins, fiber, and natural sweetness, making them a perfect snack or ingredient in desserts, smoothies, and breakfast bowls.

Berry Facts

  • Blueberries are native to North America.
  • Strawberries are the only fruit with seeds on the outside.
  • Blackberries and raspberries belong to the rose family.
