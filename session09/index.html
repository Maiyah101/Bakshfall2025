Sweet, Juicy & Vibrant

Strawberries are bright red, sweet, and bursting with flavor. They are rich in antioxidants and Vitamin C, known for promoting heart health and glowing skin.

Fun Facts

  • Strawberries are the only fruit with seeds on the outside.
  • There are over 600 varieties of strawberries worldwide.
  • They belong to the rose family!
