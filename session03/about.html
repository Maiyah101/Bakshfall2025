Overview

The Fly Agaric (Amanita muscaria) is a beautiful mushroom known for its bright red cap with white spots.

Interesting Facts

  • Toxic and should not be eaten.
  • Used in old rituals for its hallucinogenic effects.
  • Common in fairy tales and video games.

Classification

  1. Kingdom: Fungi
  2. Family: Amanitaceae
  3. Genus: Amanita
